About Me

Hey, I'm a South African-born American business magnate, investor and engineer.I am the founder, CEO, and lead designer of SpaceX; co-founder, CEO, and product architect of Tesla, Inc.; and co-founder and CEO of Neuralink. In December 2016, I was ranked 21st on the Forbes list of The World's Most Powerful People.
